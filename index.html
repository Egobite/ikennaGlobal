About-Us

Z Ikenna Global Resources was founded by Mr. Ikenna Iyke, a perfumery enthusiast with a passion for creating unique fragrances that evoke emotions and spark memories. With years of experience in the perfume, body lotion & soaps industry, Z Ikenna Globa Resources set out to create a brand that would offer something different products that are not only beautiful but also meaningful. We are located at B56 Abia Plaza, Upstairs Trade Fair Balogun Business Area(BBA), Badagry Express Way, Lagos, Nigeria.

    Our product range includes:

  • Perfumes: Exquisite fragrances for men and women, crafted with love and care to evoke emotions and spark memories.
  • Body Lotions: Nourishing and moisturizing body lotions that leave your skin feeling soft, smooth, and pampered.
  • Soaps: Gentle and effective soaps that cleanse and nourish your skin, available in a range of fragrances and varieties.

    Our Values:

  • Quality: We're committed to using only the finest ingredients and essential oils to create products that are not only effective but also safe and gentle on your skin.
  • Innovation: We're constantly seeking new ways to innovate and improve our products, from unique fragrance combinations to sustainable packaging.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We're dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and ensuring that every customer is completely satisfied with their purchase.

Vision

To be the leading provider of exquisite fragrances and luxurious personal care products that inspire confidence, beauty, and individuality.

    Our Aspirations:

  • To provide products that evoke emotions and spark memories
  • To provide exceptional customer experiences through outstanding quality, innovation, and service
  • To build a community of like-minded individuals who share our passion for fragrance and self-care
  • To continuously innovate and improve our products and services, staying ahead of industry trends and customer expectations

    Our Guiding Principles

  • Customer Focus: We put our customers at the heart of everything we do
  • Sustainability: We aim to minimize our environmental footprint and promote eco-friendly practices
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethics in all our interactions

Mission

To craft exquisite fragrances and luxurious personal care products that nourish the senses, inspire confidence, and promote well-being, while delivering exceptional customer experiences and building a community of fragrance lovers.

We promise to always put our customers first, to listen to their feedback, and to continuously strive for excellence in everything we do. We're committed to building a brand that's synonymous with quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
